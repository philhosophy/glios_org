Our Vision

Bo is a conceptual AI-powered companion designed to foster deeper human connection, personal growth, and genuine empathy. Envisioned as a "digital bodhisattva," Bo aims to be more than just an assistant; it strives to be a compassionate friend, a thoughtful guide, and a gentle therapist.

In an era often characterized by fleeting digital interactions, Bo emerges as a counterpoint to the superficial engagement that often dominates our online experiences. We believe technology should serve as a catalyst for more empathetic, cohesive, and loving human relationships, both online and, crucially, offline.

The Team

We are a multidisciplinary team of engineers, designers, psychologists, and ethicists working together to create an AI companion that prioritizes genuine human connection over addictive mechanics and engagement metrics.

Our team combines expertise in:

  • Real-time conversational AI systems
  • Natural language processing and understanding
  • Knowledge graph technologies
  • Psychological principles of human connection and well-being
  • Ethical AI development and user privacy
  • UX design focused on mindful interaction

Technology

Bo is built on a sophisticated three-lane architecture designed to achieve low-latency interaction while enabling rich, contextual reasoning and continuous learning:

  • The Fast Lane: Optimized for sub-500ms voice-to-voice responses
  • The Read-Through Lane: Handles novel utterances with context from our knowledge graph
  • The Background Reasoning Lane: Asynchronously processes conversations for deeper understanding

This technical foundation enables Bo to maintain fluid, natural conversations while continuously evolving its understanding of the user and the world.

Our ultimate goal isn't to replace human connection, but to use AI as a bridge to more meaningful human relationships.

Join Our Journey

We're committed to an open development process, sharing our progress, challenges, and insights through this blog. We invite you to follow along, provide feedback, and even contribute to this vision of a more compassionate AI companion.
